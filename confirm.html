Our Lady of Sorrows Academy is holding its Ninth Annual Jog-A-Thon on November 21, 2018, our major fundraiser for this Academic Year. Our goal is to raise $35,000 and we are relying on your generous support and prayers to succeed!

All proceeds from this yearâ€™s Jog-A-Thon will benefit the Academy, and will be used to fund, among other expenses, replacement of broken and outdated sports equipment, and replacement of broken air conditioning units in several classrooms.

In the weeks preceding the day of the Jog-A-Thon, students obtain sponsors to make a per lap pledge or a one-time donation. On the day of the event, participants will have one hour to run, jog, or walk as many times as they can around a one-eighth mile track.

Prizes will be awarded for students who run the most number of laps, as well as for students who collect the most amount of money. Additional incentive prizes are awarded throughout the campaign.

Please sponsor our students, teachers, and religious! Select the name(s) of who you are graciously sponsoring, then enter the amount of your donation, your contact information and payment information.
